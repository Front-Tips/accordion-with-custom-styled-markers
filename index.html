FarmBot How-To Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

How to set up your FarmBot
Setting up your FarmBot involves assembling the frame, attaching the motors and tracks, and installing the electronics. Ensure all components are securely fastened. Connect the FarmBot to your garden's water supply and to a power source. Finally, configure the FarmBot using the provided software, setting up the plant layout and scheduling.
Programming FarmBot for optimal Plant Growth

Program your FarmBot by using the web application to create sequences for watering, weeding, and planting. Customize these sequences based on the types of plants and their specific needs. Regularly update the plant growth stages in the app to ensure FarmBot adjusts its care routines accordingly.
